About

Live Cricket Score(LCS) is the world's leading cricket website and among the top single-sport websites in the world. Founded in 2019, LCScricinfo's content includes news, live ball-by-ball coverage of one-day international matches and features written by some of the world's best cricketers and cricket writers. The site also includes in-depth statistics on every one of the 3000 international and 50,000 first-class cricketers to have played the game. Now a wholly owned subsidiary of Lsc Inc., the world's leading multimedia sports entertainment company, LCScricinfo is available to cricket fans through the online media and on a host of mobile platforms and handheld devices.
